    深圳市萌宠当家网络科技有限公司成立于2018年，是一家专注于宠物日用品以及智能产品设计研发、生产及销售的科技企业。公司总部位于粤港澳大湾区的深圳福田，并在深圳、东莞等地设有合作工厂。公司技术和研发实力成长迅速，目前拥有４项中国大陆注册商标，１项马德里商标，１项外观设计专利，１项实用新型专利。 公司自成立以来，我们诚信经营，并始终坚持“萌宠为王”的设计理念，致力于研发适合宠物习性，减少饲主烦恼的宠物日用品以及智能产品。我们在中国也有着成熟且完善的供应链体系，与东莞、深圳等数十个工厂有着良好合作，在这里广大客户可以根据自身需求来进行一站式宠物用品采购。 我们这里也汇聚着一群充满朝气和活力的８５后，我们对宠物行业工作抱有极大的热忱，积极为客户提供最优质的产品和服务，并实现企业快速、稳定地发展。 我们也对宠物充满爱心，公司成立２年多以来，我们还积极参与流浪动物救助工作，累计向流浪动物保护组织捐献犬粮、猫粮、罐头以及其他用品累计十余次。 深圳市萌宠当家网络科技有限公司未来将继续秉持“萌宠为王”的理念，让萌宠生活更舒适，让广大新老客户更放心。 Shenzhen Tonnie Pets Network Technology Co., Ltd. was established in 2018. It is a technology enterprise focusing on the design, development, production and sales of pet daily necessities and smart products. The company is headquartered in Futian, Shenzhen, Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area, and has cooperative factories in Shenzhen, Dongguan and other places. The company's innovative technology and R&D capabilities have developed rapidly and have made some achievements. Currently, it has 4 registered trademarks in Mainland China, 1 Madrid trademark, 1 design patent and 1 utility model patent. Since its establishment, we have been operating with integrity, adhering to the design concept of "cute pet is king", and committed to researching and developing pet daily necessities and smart products that are suitable for pet habits and reduce the pet owners' trouble. In China, we have a mature and complete supply chain system, and have good cooperation with dozens of factories in Dongguan, Shenzhen, etc. Therefore, our customers can purchase one-stop pet products according to their own needs. In addition, the company also brings together a group of vigorous and energetic people post-85s generation. It’s such a group of people have great enthusiasm for pet work and actively provide customers with the best quality products and services, and achieve rapid and stable development of the enterprise. We are also full of love for pets. In the past two years, we have actively participated in stray animal rescue work. According to statistics, we have donated dog food, cat food, canned food and other supplies to stray animal protection organizations more than ten times. In the future, Shenzhen Tonnie Pets Network Technology Co., Ltd. will continue to uphold the concept of "the cute pet is king" to make the life of pets more comfortable and make the new and old customers more at ease.
